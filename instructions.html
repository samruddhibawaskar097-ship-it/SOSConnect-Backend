How To Use SOS CONNECT

How to use SOS

Tap the SOS button on the dashboard in emergency situations. The app will send alerts to your emergency contacts and activate safety features like location sharing and recording.

How to add contacts

Go to the Contacts section on the dashboard and add trusted family or friends. These contacts will receive SOS alerts when the emergency button is triggered.

Permissions required

Location – to share your live location
Camera – for emergency video recording
Microphone – for voice trigger SOS
SMS – to send alerts to contacts
